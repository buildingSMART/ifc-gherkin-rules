   005 | Feature: ALS017 - Alignment vertical segment geometric continuity
   006 | 
   007 |   The rule verifies that there is geometric continuity between segments in an IfcGradientCurve.
   008 |   The calculated end position and tangent vector of segment `n` is compared to the provided placement of segment `n + 1`.
   009 |   A warning is emitted if the calculated difference is greater than the applicable tolerance.
   010 |   The tolerance for positional and gradient continuity is taken from the precision of the applicable geometric context.
   011 |
