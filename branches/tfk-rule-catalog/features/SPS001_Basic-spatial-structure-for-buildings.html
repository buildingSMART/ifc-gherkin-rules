SPS001 Basic spatial structure for buildings - v2

001 | @implementer-agreement
002 | @SPS
003 | @version2
004 | @E00160
005 | Feature: SPS001 - Basic spatial structure for buildings
006 | The rule verifies that there's maximum one instance of IfcSite and at least one instance of IfcBuilding as part of the spatial structure.
007 |
008 |   Background:
009 |     Given A file with Model View Definition 'CoordinationView_V2.0'
010 |     Given A file with Schema 'IFC2X3'
011 |
012 |
013 |   Scenario: Agreement141 - Agreement on having maximum of one instance of IfcSite
014 |
015 |     Then There must be at most 1 instance(s) of IfcSite
016 |
017 |   Scenario: Agreement142(1) - Agreement on having at least one instance of IfcBuilding as part of the spatial structure
018 |
019 |     Then There must be at least 1 instance(s) of IfcBuilding
020 |
021 |   Scenario: Agreement142(2) - Agreement on having at least one instance of IfcBuilding as part of the spatial structure
022 |
023 |     Given An .IfcSite.
024 |     Given an .IfcBuilding.
025 |     Then It must be assigned to the IfcSite
026 |
027 |
028 |   Scenario: Agreement142(3) - Agreement on having at least one instance of IfcBuilding as part of the spatial structure
029 |
030 |   Given no .IfcSite.
031 |   Given An .IfcBuilding.
032 |
033 |   Then It must be assigned to the IfcProject
