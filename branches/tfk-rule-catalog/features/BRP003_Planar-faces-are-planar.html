005 | Feature: BRP003 - Planar faces are planar
006 | The rule verifies that polygonal faces of open and closed shells, which do not have an explicit underlying surface geometry, are planar within the tolerance measure set in the geometric representation context.
007 |
008 |   Scenario: IfcFace
009 |
010 |     Given an .IfcFace. ^without subtypes^
011 |     Then the boundaries of the face must conform to the implicit plane fitted through the boundary points
