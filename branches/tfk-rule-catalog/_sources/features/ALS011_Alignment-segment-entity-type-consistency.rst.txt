   005 | Feature: ALS011 - Alignment segment entity type consistency
   006 | 
   007 |   The rule verifies that the same entity type is used for all segments
   008 |   and that IfcCurveSegment is used with IfcSegmentedReferenceCurve and IfcGradientCurve
   009 |   The rule is similar to GEM011, except that it requires the presence of an IfcAlignment.
   010 | 
   011 | 
   012 | Background:
   013 |   :doc:`Given a model with Schema 'IFC4.3' </steps/givensattributespy_Afile_or_modelwithfieldvalues>`

   015 |   :doc:`Given its attribute .Representation. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   016 |   :doc:`Given its attribute .Representation. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   017 |   :doc:`Given its attribute .Items. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   018 | 
   019 | 
   020 | Scenario Outline: Consistent entity types used - direct representation
   021 | 
   022 |   :doc:`Given an .\<entity\>. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   023 |   :doc:`Given its attribute .Segments. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   024 |   :doc:`Given its entity type </steps/givensentitiespy_Itsentitytype>`
   025 | 
   026 |   :doc:`Then The values must be identical at depth 1 </steps/thensvaluespy_Thevaluesmustbeunique_or_identicalunique_or_identicalatdepthdepth_leveld>`
