005 | Feature: IFC102 - Absence of deprecated entities
006 |
007 | The rule verifies that the IFC model does not have deprecated entities, attributes or enumerators.
008 | By definition, a deprecated entity shall not be exported by applications.
009 | Complying interpreters shall still be able to import deprecated definitions.
010 | IFC2X3: https://standards.buildingsmart.org/IFC/RELEASE/IFC2x3/TC1/HTML/deprecated_constructs.htm
