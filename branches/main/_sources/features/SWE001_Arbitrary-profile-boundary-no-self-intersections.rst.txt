   004 | Feature: SWE001 - Arbitrary profile boundary no self-intersections
   005 | The rule verifies that IfcArbitraryClosedProfileDefs and IfcArbitraryProfileDefWithVoids do
   006 | not have any self-intersections in their boundaries. Profile definitions are the basis of
   007 | geometrical sweeps such as extrusions.
   008 | 
   009 |   Scenario: Validating that IfcArbitraryClosedProfileDef instances do not have self-intersections in their boundaries
   010 | 
   011 |     :doc:`Given An .IfcArbitraryClosedProfileDef. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   012 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .OuterCurve. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   013 | 
   014 |     :doc:`Then There must be no self-intersections </steps/thensgeometrypy_Theremustbenoselfintersections>`
   015 | 
   016 |   Scenario: Validating that IfcArbitraryProfileDefWithVoids instances do not have self-intersections in their inner boundaries
   017 | 
   018 |     :doc:`Given An .IfcArbitraryClosedProfileDef. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   019 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .InnerCurves. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   020 | 
   021 |     :doc:`Then There must be no self-intersections </steps/thensgeometrypy_Theremustbenoselfintersections>`
