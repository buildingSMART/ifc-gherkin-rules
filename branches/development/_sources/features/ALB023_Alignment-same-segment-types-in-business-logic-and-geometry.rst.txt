   005 | Feature: ALB023 - Alignment same segment types in business logic and geometry
   006 |   The rule verifies that when an Alignment has both business logic and geometry (representation),
   007 |   the geometry type of each segments in the business logic must be the same as its corresponding segment in the representation.
