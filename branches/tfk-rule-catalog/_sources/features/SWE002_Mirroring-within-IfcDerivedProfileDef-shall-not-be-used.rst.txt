``SWE002`` Mirroring within IfcDerivedProfileDef shall not be used
========================================================================================================================================================================================================

.. parsed-literal::

   001 | \@informal-proposition
   002 | \@SWE
   003 | \@version1
   004 | \@E00050
   005 | Feature: SWE002 - Mirroring within IfcDerivedProfileDef shall not be used
   006 | 
   007 | The rule verifies that the transformation defined in IfcDerivedProfileDef.Operator does not introduce mirroring.
   008 | The subtype IfcMirroredProfileDef should be used for that. For the tapered sweeps, which tend to rely on
   009 | IfcDerivedProfileDef, by expressing the tapering operation as a change in profile, mirroring should not be used
   010 | altogether.
   011 | 
   012 |   Scenario: IfcDerivedProfileDef must not use mirroring as there is a dedicated subtype for that
   013 | 
   014 |     Given An .IfcDerivedProfileDef. without subtypes
   015 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .Operator. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   016 |     :doc:`Given The determinant of the placement matrix </steps/givensvaluespy_Thedeterminantoftheplacementmatrix>`
   017 | 
   018 |     :doc:`Then The resulting value must be ^greater than 0^ </steps/thensattributespy_Theresultingvaluemustbevalue_or_comparison_op>`
   019 | 
   020 |   Scenario Outline: Tapered sweeps must not use mirroring altogether
   021 | 
   022 |     :doc:`Given An .\<entity\>. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   023 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .\<attribute\>. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   024 |     :doc:`Given [Its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcDerivedProfileDef' or 'IfcMirroredProfileDef' </steps/stepsattribute_valuepy_attributecomparison_operatorequal_or_not_equalvalue>`
   025 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .Operator. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   026 |     :doc:`Given The determinant of the placement matrix </steps/givensvaluespy_Thedeterminantoftheplacementmatrix>`
   027 | 
   028 |     :doc:`Then The resulting value must be ^greater than 0^ </steps/thensattributespy_Theresultingvaluemustbevalue_or_comparison_op>`
   029 | 
   030 |       Examples:
   031 |         | entity                      | attribute    |
   032 |         | IfcExtrudedAreaSolidTapered | SweptArea    |
   033 |         | IfcExtrudedAreaSolidTapered | EndSweptArea |
   034 |         | IfcRevolvedAreaSolidTapered | SweptArea    |
   035 |         | IfcRevolvedAreaSolidTapered | EndSweptArea |
