   004 | Feature: ALB032 - Alignment Layouts Reusing Horizontal
   005 | The rule verifies that alignments are nested in accordance with Concept Template 4.1.4.4.1.2
   006 | which represents the case of a horizontal layout being reused by vertical layouts.
   007 | Alignment layouts are abbreviated as follows: horiz = IfcAlignmentHorizontal, vert = IfcAlignmentVertical,
   008 | and cant = IfcAlignmentCant
   009 | 
   010 | Background:
   011 |     :doc:`Given A model with Schema 'IFC4.3' </steps/givensattributespy_Afile_or_modelwithfieldvalues>`
   012 |     :doc:`Given an .IfcAlignment. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   013 | 
   014 | Scenario: Agreement on IfcAlignment layout nesting per 4.1.4.4.1.2 for "parent" alignment
   015 | 
   016 |     :doc:`Given a relationship .IfcRelAggregates. from .IfcAlignment. to .IfcAlignment. </steps/givensrelationshipspy_Arelationshiprelationshipdir1from_toentitydir2from_toother_entity>`
   017 |     :doc:`Given a relationship .IfcRelNests. from .IfcAlignment. to .IfcObject. </steps/givensrelationshipspy_Arelationshiprelationshipdir1from_toentitydir2from_toother_entity>`
   018 |     :doc:`Then the alignment layouts must include [1 horiz] </steps/thensexistencepy_thealignmentlayoutsmustincludeprose_matching>`
   019 |

   021 |
