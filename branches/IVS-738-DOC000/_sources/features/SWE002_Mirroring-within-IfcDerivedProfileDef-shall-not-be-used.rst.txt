``SWE002`` Mirroring within IfcDerivedProfileDef shall not be used - v3
========================================================================================================================================================================================================

.. parsed-literal::

   001 | \@informal-proposition
   002 | \@SWE
   003 | \@version3
   004 | Feature: SWE002 - Mirroring within IfcDerivedProfileDef shall not be used
   005 | 
   006 | The rule verifies that the transformation defined in IfcDerivedProfileDef.Operator does not introduce mirroring.
   007 | The subtype IfcMirroredProfileDef should be used for that. For the tapered sweeps, which tend to rely on
   008 | IfcDerivedProfileDef, by expressing the tapering operation as a change in profile, mirroring should not be used
   009 | altogether.
   010 | 
   011 |   Scenario: IfcDerivedProfileDef must not use mirroring as there is a dedicated subtype for that
   012 | 
   013 |     :doc:`Given A model with Schema 'IFC4' or 'IFC4.3' </steps/givensattributespy_Afile_or_modelwithfieldvalues>`
   014 | 
   015 |     :doc:`Given An .IfcDerivedProfileDef. ^without subtypes^ </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmtsubtype_handling>`
   016 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .Operator. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   017 |     :doc:`Given The determinant of the placement matrix </steps/givensvaluespy_Thedeterminantoftheplacementmatrix>`
   018 | 
   019 |     :doc:`Then The resulting value must be ^greater than 0^ </steps/thensattributespy_Theresultingvaluemustbevalue_or_comparison_op>`
   020 | 
   021 |   Scenario Outline: Tapered sweeps must not use mirroring altogether
   022 | 
   023 |     :doc:`Given An .\<entity\>. </steps/stepsentity_selectionpy_Anentity_opt_stmt>`
   024 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .\<attribute\>. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   025 |     :doc:`Given [Its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcDerivedProfileDef' or 'IfcMirroredProfileDef' </steps/stepsattribute_valuepy_attributecomparison_operatorequal_or_not_equalvalue>`
   026 |     :doc:`Given Its attribute .Operator. </steps/stepsattribute_selectionpy_Itsattributeattribute>`
   027 |     :doc:`Given The determinant of the placement matrix </steps/givensvaluespy_Thedeterminantoftheplacementmatrix>`
   028 | 
   029 |     :doc:`Then The resulting value must be ^greater than 0^ </steps/thensattributespy_Theresultingvaluemustbevalue_or_comparison_op>`
   030 | 
   031 |       Examples:
   032 |         | entity                      | attribute    |
   033 |         | IfcExtrudedAreaSolidTapered | SweptArea    |
   034 |         | IfcExtrudedAreaSolidTapered | EndSweptArea |
   035 |         | IfcRevolvedAreaSolidTapered | SweptArea    |
   036 |         | IfcRevolvedAreaSolidTapered | EndSweptArea |
