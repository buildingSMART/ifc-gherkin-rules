   001 | \@IFC

   003 | \@E00030
   004 | \@implementer-agreement
   005 | Feature: IFC102 - Absence of deprecated entities
   006 | 
   007 | The rule verifies that the IFC model does not have deprecated entities, attributes or enumerators.
   008 | By definition, a deprecated entity shall not be exported by applications.
   009 | Complying interpreters shall still be able to import deprecated definitions.
   010 | IFC2X3: https://standards.buildingsmart.org/IFC/RELEASE/IFC2x3/TC1/HTML/deprecated\_constructs.htm
   011 | IFC4X3: https://standards.buildingsmart.org/IFC/RELEASE/IFC4\_3/HTML/content/introduction.htm#List-of-known-backward-incompatibilities-of-this-document-with-ISO-16739-1-2018
   012 | IFC4: https://standards.buildingsmart.org/IFC/RELEASE/IFC4/ADD2\_TC1/HTML/
