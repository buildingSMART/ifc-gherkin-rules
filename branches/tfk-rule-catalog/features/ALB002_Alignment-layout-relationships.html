ALB002 Alignment layout relationships

001 | @implementer-agreement
002 | @ALB
003 | @version1
004 | @E00100
005 |
006 | Feature: ALB002 - Alignment Layout Relationships
007 |   The rule verifies that nesting and decomposition relationships are used correctly with alignment layouts.
008 |
009 |
010 |   Background:
011 |
012 |     Given A model with Schema 'IFC4.3'
013 |
014 |   Scenario: IfcAlignment can only be decomposed by other "children" IfcAlignment instances
015 |
016 |     Given an .IfcAlignment.
017 |     Given its attribute .IsDecomposedBy.
018 |     Given its attribute .RelatedObjects.
019 |
020 |     Then [its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcAlignment'
021 |
022 |
023 |   Scenario Outline: Agreement of structure of alignment segments
024 |
025 |     Given an .<entity>.
026 |     Given its attribute .IsNestedBy.
027 |     Given its attribute .RelatedObjects.
028 |
029 |     Then [its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcAlignmentSegment'
030 |
031 |       Examples:
032 |       | entity                  |
033 |       | IfcAlignmentHorizontal  |
034 |       | IfcAlignmentVertical    |
035 |       | IfcAlignmentCant        |
036 |
037 |   Scenario Outline: Agreement of the segments of alignment
038 |
039 |     Given an .<entity>.
040 |     Given its attribute .IsNestedBy.
041 |     Given its attribute .RelatedObjects.
042 |     Given its attribute .DesignParameters.
043 |
044 |     Then [its entity type] ^is^ '<SegmentType>'
045 |
046 |       Examples:
047 |       | entity                  | SegmentType                       |
048 |       | IfcAlignmentHorizontal  | IfcAlignmentHorizontalSegment     |
049 |       | IfcAlignmentVertical    | IfcAlignmentVerticalSegment       |
050 |       | IfcAlignmentCant        | IfcAlignmentCantSegment           |
