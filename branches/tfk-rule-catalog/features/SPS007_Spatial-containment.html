SPS007 Spatial containment

001 | @implementer-agreement
002 | @SPS
003 | @version6
004 | @E00040
005 |
006 | Feature: SPS007 - Spatial Containment
007 | The rule verifies that spatial containment via IfcRelContainedInSpatialStructure is utilised in accordance with Concept Template for Spatial Containment
008 |
009 |
010 |     Scenario: Instances of IfcGrid must be contained within a spatial structure
011 |
012 |         Given an .IfcGrid.
013 |         Then a relationship .IfcRelContainedInSpatialStructure. ^must exist^ to .IfcGrid. from .IfcSpatialStructureElement.
014 |
015 |
016 |     Scenario: Instances of IfcAnnotation must be contained within a spatial structure, except when the annotation is nested under another annotation
017 |
018 |         Given an .IfcAnnotation.
019 |         Given A relationship .IfcRelNests. ^does not exist^ to .IfcAnnotation. from .IfcAnnotation.
020 |
021 |         Then a relationship .IfcRelContainedInSpatialStructure. ^must exist^ to .IfcAnnotation. from .IfcSpatialStructureElement.
022 |
023 |
024 |     Scenario: Instances of IfcElement must be part of a spatial structure, with certain exceptions
025 |
026 |         Given an .IfcElement.
027 |         Given [Its Type] ^is not^ 'IfcFeatureElement' ^including subtypes^
028 |         Given .Decomposes. ^is^ empty
029 |
030 |         Then a *required* relationship .IfcRelContainedInSpatialStructure. to .IfcElement. from .IfcSpatialStructureElement.
031 |
032 |
033 |     Scenario: Entities that are an aggregated part of another element must not also be part of a spatial structure
034 |         Given an .IfcElement.
035 |         Given a relationship .IfcRelAggregates. to .IfcElement. from .IfcElement.
036 |
037 |         Then .ContainedInStructure. ^is^ empty
038 |
039 |
040 |     Scenario: All other IFC entities must not be contained within a spatial structure
041 |         Given An .IfcProduct.
042 |         Given [Its Type] ^is not^ 'IfcElement' ^including subtypes^
043 |         Given [Its Type] ^is not^ 'IfcGrid'
044 |         Given [Its Type] ^is not^ 'IfcAnnotation'
045 |
046 |         Then .ContainedInStructure. ^is^ empty
