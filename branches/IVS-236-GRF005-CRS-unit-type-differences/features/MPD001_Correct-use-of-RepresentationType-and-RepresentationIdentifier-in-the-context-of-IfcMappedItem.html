004 | Feature: MPD001 - Correct use of RepresentationType and RepresentationIdentifier in the context of IfcMappedItem
005 | The rule verifies that the shape representations being mapped as part of an IfcMappedItem all have the same
006 | RepresentationType and that the RepresentationIdentifier correspond to that of the representation that owns the mapped item.
007 |
008 |   Scenario: Agreement on the equality of RepresentationIdentifier
009 |
010 |       Given an .IfcShapeRepresentation.
011 |       Given its attribute .RepresentationIdentifier. [stored as 'ParentIdentifier']
012 |       Given the instances '2' steps up
013 |       Given its attribute .Items.
014 |       Given [its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcMappedItem'
015 |       Given its attribute .MappingSource.
016 |       Given its attribute .MappedRepresentation.
017 |       Given its attribute .RepresentationIdentifier. [stored as 'ChildIdentifier']
018 |
019 |       Then the value 'ChildIdentifier' must be ^equal to^ the value 'ParentIdentifier'
020 |
021 |   Scenario: Agreement on the equality of RepresentationType
022 |
023 |       Given an .IfcShapeRepresentation.
024 |       Given its attribute .Items.
025 |       Given [its entity type] ^is^ 'IfcMappedItem'
026 |       Given its attribute .MappingSource.
027 |       Given its attribute .MappedRepresentation.
028 |       Given its attribute .RepresentationType.
029 |
030 |       Then the values must be identical at depth 1
