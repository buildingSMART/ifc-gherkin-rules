   005 | Feature: GRF000 - Georeferencing
   006 | The rule verifies the presence of IFC entities used to define the geographic location and orientation of the model relative to a coordinate reference system.
   007 | The simple attributes on IfcSite: RefLatitude, RefLongitude and RefElevation are not considered proper georeferencing in the context of this rule.
